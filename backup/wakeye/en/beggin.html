Prologue. The origin of the seal of Hikarikagaya Monster


Once upon a time, when Tiantian-chan was creating humanity,
he accidentally lost the monster stone.
It is heard that people saw the monster stone split into
Hikarikagaya Monster and five mysterious magical stones.
Hikarikagaya Monster roamed around the world, causing havoc.
Legends say that the five stones are enclosed
within a black box on the peak of AHAHAHAH mountain,
where they would duplicate at times.
It is believed that, if anybody is able to take the magical stones
out of the box and put them together, Hikarikagaya Monster
will be sealed again, and humanity will no longer be under chaos.
